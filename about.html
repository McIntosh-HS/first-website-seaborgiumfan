ABOUT US!

Snug as a Bug in a Rug

We are quite an eclectic business. We sell the finest selection of rugs, new and old, and from all over the world! If you're looking for a great conversation piece or some real nice decor, come give us a visit. Some of tthe finest antique rugs can be found at Snug as a Bug in a Rug, and if you have something we can refurbish OR buy it!
In addition to rugs, we sell bugs. Big and small, you can find your new best friend. We specialize in beetles and have many other domestic, yet friendly, bugs to snuggle up on your new rug. It's a package deal! Get two in one!
At Snug as a Bug in a Rug we aim to give to the community by providing an excellent selection of bugs, rugs, and antiques you can't find anywhere else. Located in Peachtree City, GA, we exist as the first store ever to sell bugs and rugs at the same location. Many of our customers have amazing experiences with us. We have a great warranty process and on-site rug cleaners. Come support a local business with rug artists, the finest selection of textiles you have ever seen, cute insect friends, and trinkets you can't wait to get your hands on...
